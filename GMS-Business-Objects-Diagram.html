System Overview

Complete system architecture and user roles - 38 total views across 5 portals

User Roles & Portals

Portal User Role Primary Functions Views

Reporting Team CTC Internal Staff Generate reports, track compliance, review submitted reports 6 views

Complete Application Lifecycle

1. IHE Submits Candidate
IHE enters candidate info via manual entry or bulk upload. Candidate status: PENDING
2. LEA Reviews & Confirms
LEA reviews candidate, confirms partnership, provides Application Info (contacts, payment info). Adds to submission batch.
3. CTC Grants Team Reviews
Grants Team reviews submission. Can: APPROVE WAITLIST or DENY
4. Budget Check
If budget available -> Approve. If budget exhausted -> Waitlist. Waitlisted candidates promoted when funds released.
5. Disbursement Processing (6 Steps)
GAA Signed -> PO Generated -> PO Uploaded -> Invoice Generated -> Sent to Accounting -> Warrant Confirmed
6. LEA Signs GAA
DocuSign integration sends GAA to LEA's Authorized Signer for signature.
7. Payment Processing
Fiscal Team generates PO, uploads to Fi$Cal, generates invoice, sends to accounting, confirms warrant.
8. Post-Payment Reporting
Both IHE and LEA submit outcome reports. IHE: program completion, hours, credential. LEA: payment details, employment.

Candidate Status Flow

PENDING -> UNDER_REVIEW -> APPROVED -> PAID

Alternative paths: PENDING -> WAITLIST (if no budget) -> APPROVED (when funds available). Any status -> DENIED.

6-Step Disbursement Workflow

1 GAA Signed
2 PO Generated
3 PO Uploaded
4 Invoice Generated
5 Sent to Accounting
6 Warrant Confirmed
Internal States: NEEDS_GAA -> NEEDS_PO -> NEEDS_INVOICE -> NEEDS_WARRANT -> COMPLETE
